About Dominion Review Pro

Dominion Review Pro is an automated Google review management service dedicated to helping local businesses across the United States get more 5-star reviews, rank higher on Google Maps, and attract more customers — all on autopilot.

Our Mission

We believe every great local business deserves to be found online. Most business owners deliver excellent service but struggle to turn that into visible Google reviews. We built Dominion Review Pro to solve that problem permanently — with automation that runs 24/7 so you never have to think about it.

Our system sends personalized, FTC-compliant review requests to your customers via SMS and email right after their visit — when they're most likely to leave a glowing review.

Why We Built This

We saw too many great local businesses getting outranked by inferior competitors — simply because those competitors had a system for collecting reviews and most didn't. Google reviews are the single most important factor in local search ranking. We leveled the playing field.

Part of the Dominion Family

Dominion Review Pro is part of the Dominion brand family — a group of AI-powered business services based in East Texas, dedicated to helping local businesses compete and win in the digital age.
