Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legal? Can I get penalized by Google?

Yes, this is completely legal and Google-compliant. We send review requests to your actual customers — we never buy fake reviews or incentivize reviews in a way that violates Google's policies. Our system follows FTC guidelines and Google's Terms of Service.

How long until I see results?

Most businesses see their first new reviews within the first week. Significant ranking improvements typically show within 60-90 days of consistent review generation. The more transactions you have, the faster you'll see results.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

No contracts required. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We're confident you'll stay because the results speak for themselves.

What types of businesses does this work for?

Any local business that serves customers works great — restaurants, salons, auto shops, contractors, medical offices, retail stores, gyms, and more. If you have customers, we can get you reviews.

How do I add customers to the system?

It's simple — after each transaction, you enter the customer's phone number (and email on Pro/Agency plans) into our system. That's it. The automated sequence handles everything from there.

What happens with negative reviews?

On Pro and Agency plans, you receive an instant alert when a negative review is posted so you can respond quickly. Our system also helps you respond professionally to turn negative experiences into positive impressions.
